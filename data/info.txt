The data for used during the sentiment analysis can be found in kaggle: 
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/miguelaenlle/massive-stock-news-analysis-db-for-nlpbacktests/data

Because the files were to large, they could not be uploaded to GitHub. Note that
once downloading the data, the data should be stored in the current folder 'data' 
since this data will be read during the analysis.
